Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Game Life is a personal progress-tracking app. This page explains what data the app collects, how it's stored, and how you can delete it.

What we collect

  • Account info: if you choose to create an account, your email address and password are handled entirely by Supabase Auth (our authentication provider) — Game Life never sees or stores your password.
  • Gameplay data: everything you enter yourself — your Hunter name, the habits/dailies/quests/rewards you create, and the actions you log. There is no data collected about you beyond what you explicitly type or tap into the app.
  • Nothing else: no location, no contacts, no device identifiers, no analytics SDKs, no advertising IDs.

How it's stored

Your gameplay data lives locally on your device first, for a fast, fully-offline experience. If you sign in, an optional encrypted backup of that same data is stored in a Postgres database (hosted by Supabase), isolated to your account with row-level security — no other user, including us, can query your rows through the app's normal access path.

What we don't do

  • No ads, ever.
  • No third-party trackers or analytics.
  • No selling, renting, or sharing your data with anyone.

Deleting your data

You can permanently delete your account and all associated cloud data at any time from Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account inside the app. See Account & Data Deletion for full details on what gets removed.

If you never created an account, your data only ever existed on your own device — uninstalling the app removes it completely.
