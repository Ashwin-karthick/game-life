Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: July 2026

Game Life supports deleting your account and all of its data directly from within the app — no separate web form or support request is needed.

How to delete your account

  1. Open Game Life and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Scroll to Danger Zone.
  4. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently and immediately removes:

  • Your authentication record (email, login credentials) from Supabase Auth.
  • Your profile, attributes, and settings.
  • Every quest, habit, daily, reward, and inventory item you created.
  • Your entire journal of logged actions and progression events.
  • Your weekly challenge history.

This action cannot be undone. There is no recovery window or backup retention after deletion.

If you never created an account

If you've only used Game Life as a guest (no sign-in), none of your data was ever sent anywhere — it lives only on your device. Uninstalling the app or using Settings → Reset All Data removes it completely.

Contact
